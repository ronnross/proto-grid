What about rows

We can have a row span two rows

span 11
span 4 row-span-2 I now span two rows
span 11

Rows go upto 15. You can also collapse a row using .row-span-0

What about responsive?

default is mobile. You can add a -md/-lg modifier for medium/large screens respectivly.

6 small / 9 medium / 12 large

this works for all other classes like grid-gap and row-span
